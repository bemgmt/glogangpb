GLO GANG Photobooth Help

Welcome to the GLO GANG Photobooth! This guide will help you get started and troubleshoot common issues.

Getting Started

1 Access the Photobooth

Click "Click here to start" on the home page to begin your session.

2 Authentication

Choose between QR code scanning or payment options to access the photobooth.

3 Take Photos

Use the camera interface to capture photos with overlays and digital props.

4 Save & Share

Save your photos to the device or share them via AirDrop, Bluetooth, or other sharing options.

Features

Camera Controls

Switch between front and back cameras, toggle mirror mode, and adjust aspect ratios (3:4, 1:1, 9:16).

Overlays

Add full-frame overlays like frames and effects to your photos. Upload custom overlays or use presets.

Digital Props

Add interactive stickers and props. Drag to move, pinch to resize and rotate. Tap to select and delete.

QR Code Access

Scan approved QR codes for quick access, or enter codes manually if camera scanning isn't available.

Troubleshooting

Camera Issues

Camera not working: Ensure you've granted camera permissions and are using HTTPS. Try refreshing the page.

Poor image quality: Make sure you have good lighting and the camera lens is clean.

QR Code Scanning

QR code not scanning: Ensure good lighting, hold the code steady, and try manual entry if scanning fails.

Code not recognized: Verify the QR code is approved. Contact staff if you believe it should work.

Photo Saving

Can't save photos: Check if your device has sufficient storage space. Clear browser data if needed.

Sharing not working: Ensure your device supports the Web Share API or try downloading the photo instead.

Tips for Best Results

Lighting: Use good lighting for the best photo quality. Avoid backlighting.

Positioning: Center yourself in the frame and maintain a comfortable distance from the camera.

Props: Experiment with different combinations of overlays and props for creative effects.

Aspect Ratios: Choose the right aspect ratio for your intended use (social media, printing, etc.).

Staff Settings

Staff can access advanced settings by clicking the settings icon on the home page. The default PIN is 1234.

Available settings:

  • Enable/disable payment requirements
  • Manage photo gallery
  • Adjust account credits
  • Change settings PIN

Browser Compatibility

For the best experience, use a modern browser with camera and JavaScript support:

  • Recommended: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge (latest versions)
  • Required: Camera access, JavaScript enabled
  • Optional: Web Share API for enhanced sharing features

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is important to us:

  • Photos are stored locally on your device
  • No photos are uploaded to external servers
  • Clear browser data to remove all stored photos
  • Session data expires automatically for security

Need More Help?

If you're still experiencing issues, please contact the event staff or technical support.

For technical issues, please note your browser type and version, device model, and a description of the problem.
